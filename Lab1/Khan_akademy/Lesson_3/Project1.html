Pasta Carbonara

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or guanciale 150g
Eggs 4
Grated Pecorino Romano cheese 1 cup
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Cook pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with egg mixture.
  5. Add cooked pancetta or guanciale and toss until well combined.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Makes: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
Unsalted butter, softened 1 cup
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar, packed 3/4 cup
Large eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Semisweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix flour and baking soda.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture, then fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets and bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
